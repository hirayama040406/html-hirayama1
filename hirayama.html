The 1st reason why did I chose BSIT

First of all, I chose BSIT because I have always loved playing video games and dreamed of becoming a game developer.
Since I was young, I was fascinated by how games are made and how the characters and stories come to life.
Instead of just playing games, I wanted to learn how to create them myself.
BSIT offers the chance to develop the skills needed to make games, and it seemed like the best choice for turning my passion into a career.

THE 2nd reason why did I chose BSIT

One of the main reasons I chose BSIT is because it teaches important skills for game development, such as programming and software design.
Learning how to code is essential for creating games, and BSIT provides the knowledge to work with different programming languages and game development tools.
These skills will help me understand how to build the logic behind games and create the mechanics that make them fun and engaging for players.

The 3rd reason why did I chose BSIT

BSIT also covers areas that are important for making a complete game, such as game design, graphics, and animation.
It is not just about writing code but it is also about making the game look good and sound great.
The course gives me the chance to learn both technical and creative skills, which will help me design not only the gameplay but also the visual and audio elements of a game.
This well-rounded knowledge is crucial for becoming a successful game developer.

The last reason why did I Chose BSIT

Lastly, I chose BSIT because the gaming industry is growing fast, and there is a high demand for game developers.
With the rise of mobile games, virtual reality, and online gaming, there are more opportunities than ever before.
I believe that by studying BSIT, I will be well-prepared to join this exciting field and create games that people around the world will enjoy.
Becoming a game developer is my dream, and BSIT is the first step toward making that dream come true.
